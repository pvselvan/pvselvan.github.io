A Page From My Story

A little about me...

I've always been fascinated by stories. As a reader, a writer, and an (amateur) photographer, I'm constantly curious about the world around us and enjoy learning more every day.

I believe code is a tool that lets you write your story with technology. It is a way for us to bring our ideas to life in a very powerful and creative way. I code, I create, in hopes that I will inspire a generation of women to pursue this field and succeed.

I never once thought I'd end up halfway across the country at The University of Michigan. I thought it even less likely that I'd pursue Computer Science. After two years, it's hard to see myself doing anything else. Finding the intersection between social impact and technology has given me a fierce determination to change the world... one line of code at a time!
